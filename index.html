  trigger: "email.attachment.received",

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need technical knowledge to use Trublshut? +
No. We handle all the technical setup. Your team just needs to know how to use the tools they already use — we automate the rest behind the scenes.
How long does deployment take? +
Starter projects typically go live in 2 weeks. Pro packages take 4 weeks. Enterprise timelines vary by scope. We always agree a timeline before starting.
What tools do you work with? +
We work with n8n, Power Automate, UiPath, OpenAI, Microsoft 365, Supabase, Zapier, HubSpot, Xero, and most modern business tools. If you use it, we can likely automate it.
What happens after the project is done? +
Every package includes post-deployment support (30 or 90 days depending on plan). After that, you can retain us on a monthly basis or manage independently — your automations are fully documented and yours to own.
Can I start with a smaller scope and expand later? +
Absolutely. Most clients start with a Starter project to prove ROI, then expand to Pro or Enterprise within 90 days. We design everything to be modular and scalable from day one.
