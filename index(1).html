n8n workflow · invoice-automation.json
// UC-04: Automated Invoice Processing
const workflow = {
  trigger: "email.attachment.received",
  filter: { subject: /invoice|INV/i },
  steps: [
    extractData({ engine: "gpt-4o", fields: ["amount", "vendor", "due_date"] }),
    validateAgainstPO({ source: "supabase" }),
    postToAccounting({ system: "xero", auto_approve: true }),
    notifyTeam({ channel: "#finance", via: "slack" })
  ],
  onError: escalateToHuman({ email: "hello@trublshut.com" })
}
// Result: 14 hrs/week saved · ROI break-even: 2.8 weeks

FAQ

Common questions

Will automation replace my employees? +
Never. Our philosophy is simple: we get the jobs out of the employees, not the employees out of the jobs. Automation removes the repetitive, draining work — freeing your team to focus on relationship-building, creative problem-solving, and the strategic work that only humans can do well.
How long before we see results? +
Most clients see measurable time savings within the first 2 weeks of deployment. ROI break-even typically happens between week 2 and week 6 depending on the use case and plan. We track this with you throughout the engagement.
Do we need to change our existing tools? +
Rarely. We build around what you already use. Whether that's Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, HubSpot, Xero, or bespoke systems — we integrate with your existing stack rather than replacing it.
What makes Trublshut different from other automation agencies? +
Most agencies build and leave. We build, train, and scale with you. Every engagement includes full documentation, team training, and a growth roadmap. We're invested in your long-term results — not just the delivery milestone.
